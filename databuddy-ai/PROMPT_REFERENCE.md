## 💡 Tips for Best Results

1. **One Command at a Time**: Send separate prompts for each operation
   - ✅ "Create products table" → then "Add products" → then "Show products"
   - ❌ "Create table and add data and show me" (too many commands at once)

2. **Be Specific**: Include table names when possible
   - ✅ "Add email to employees table"
   - ❌ "Add email"

3. **Use Natural Language**: Speak like you're talking to a person
   - ✅ "Show me all employees"
   - ❌ "SELECT * FROM employees"

4. **Include Details**: More context = better results
   - ✅ "Add employee: name John, email john@email.com, salary 50000"
   - ❌ "Add John"

5. **Mention Table Names**: Helps avoid confusion
   - ✅ "Add column to employees table"
   - ❌ "Add column"

6. **Use Common Words**: 
   - "show", "get", "find", "list" for viewing
   - "add", "insert", "create" for adding
   - "change", "update", "modify" for updating
   - "remove", "delete", "clear" for removing
